GFTV · HelloQueue

Virtual Queue Management for Furry Events

Skip the physical line. Attendees join via Telegram or web, wander freely, and get notified when it's their turn. Operators manage everything from a single dashboard.

Operator Login
Telegram Native
Join queues and receive alerts directly in Telegram via @GFTVHelloQueueBot. No app installation required.
Live Display Screen
Put a display at the entrance showing numbers being served, waiting counts, and a scannable QR to join.
Animated QR Entry
When called, attendees get a one-time animated QR code on their phone to show at the door - scanned by staff to confirm entry.
Smart Notifications
Attendees choose when to be notified - when called, or when they're next in line. Both are on by default.
Role-Based Access
Admins create events and assign editors. Queue creators manage their own queues and operators.
Fast & Offline-Ready
A PWA built for convention environments with spotty WiFi. Works great on both phones and tablets.
