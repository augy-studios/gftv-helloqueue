Live Display Screen
Put a display at the entrance showing numbers being served, waiting counts, and a scannable QR to join.

Animated QR Entry
When called, attendees get a one-time animated QR code on their phone to show at the door — scanned by staff to confirm entry.
🔔
Smart Notifications
Attendees choose when to be notified — when called, or when they're next in line. Both are on by default.
🧑‍💼
Role-Based Access
Admins create events and assign editors. Queue creators manage their own queues and operators.
